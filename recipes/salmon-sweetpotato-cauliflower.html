Salmon with sweet potato and cauliflower

Home

Description

This is one of my favorite dinners ever. A nice cooked salmon with some spiced sweet potatoes and cauliflower.

It's perfect for those days where you're craving something healthy but tasty. It's also perfect if you want to avoid having to clean many things afterwards, since you'll only need two oven trays!

Ingredients

For two people:

  • Two fillets of salmon
  • Large sweet potato
  • Cauliflower
  • 1tbsp of olive oil
  • 2tsp of paprika
  • 1/2tsp of garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp of onion powder
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200º and cut the sweet potato in cubes while the oven heats.
  2. In a sheet pan, toss the sweet potato with the olive oil and add 1tsp of paprika, 1/4 tsp of garlic powder and another 1/4tsp of onion powder. Add a pinch of salt and some pepper, and put into the oven.
  3. In another sheet pan, add the cauliflower with the rest of the seasoning, and also add a pinch of salt.
  4. Put the salmon fillets in the same sheet pan as the cauliflower, and rub some salt and pepper on the salmon.
  5. Bake everything for 12-15 minutes, and serve!
